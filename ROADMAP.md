## Conceptual Mindset Shift from GR to QFT

- **GR:** Geometry determines dynamics  
- **QFT:** Quantum fluctuations define particles  
- **GR:** Fields shape spacetime  
- **QFT:** Fields *are* operators  
- **GR:** Deterministic equations  
- **QFT:** Probabilistic amplitudes
